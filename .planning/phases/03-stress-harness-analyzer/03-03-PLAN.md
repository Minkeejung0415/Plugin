<objective>
Turn the successful ICM SPI bring-up into a measured, documented no-SD acquisition ceiling.

The hardware now responds over SPI when CS is D7/GPIO44, and the AK09916 magnetometer is
reachable through the ICM aux bus. The previous I2C bottleneck is gone, but the new
payload-duplicate detector shows a different limit: above the safe rate, the firmware can
emit frames faster than the sensor/filter path produces unique readings. This plan makes
that limit explicit, tunes the ICM output-rate registers if needed, and records the no-SD
operating envelope without making any SD-card reliability claim.
</objective>
